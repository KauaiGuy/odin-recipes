Métis Bannock!

Follow this recipe for a yummy bannock recipe!

This bannock recipe has been handed down from generations of Métis people! Great for all ages!

Ingredients

  • Flour: 4 1/2 cups
  • Baking Powder: 2 tsp
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Tepid Water: 2 cups
  • Melted Lard:1 cup

Steps

  1. Mix first three ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make a well in the middle of the mixture.
  3. Add lard and water into well.
  4. Slowly mix from the middle.
  5. Knead about ten times.
  6. Flatten and pierce with a fork or spoon.
  7. Place in a lightly greased frying pan (cast iron is better).
  8. Bake in the oven at 450F for 20 to 30 minutes and enjoy!
